🏃 Count Masters

Build the biggest stickman army!

How to Play

  • 🖱 Move your mouse left and right to steer your army
  • ⬅➡ Or use arrow keys to move the crowd
  • 🟢 Go through green gates to multiply or add stickmen
  • 🔴 Avoid red gates — they shrink your army!
  • ⚔️ At the end, your army battles the enemy crowd
  • 🏆 Bring more stickmen than the enemy to win!
